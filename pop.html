You got Pop!

Pop music is a genre of popular music, which originated in its modern form in the Western world during the 1950s and 1960s, deriving from rock and roll. The terms "popular music" and "pop music" are often used interchangeably, although the former is a description of music which is popular (and can include any style). Pop is great to listen to with friends and is filled with various types of artists and styles within the amazing genre.
